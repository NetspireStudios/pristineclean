Vacation Rental Cleaning Checklist

Kitchen

  • Clean and sanitize all surfaces
  • Wipe down appliances inside and out
  • Stock clean dishes and utensils
  • Check and restock basic supplies
  • Clean coffee maker and filters
  • Sanitize sink and faucet
  • Mop floors and clean baseboards
  • Empty and clean trash cans

Bathrooms

  • Deep clean shower and tub
  • Sanitize toilet inside and out
  • Polish mirrors and fixtures
  • Restock towels and toiletries
  • Clean and disinfect all surfaces
  • Mop floors and clean baseboards
  • Check and replace amenities

Bedrooms

  • Change all linens and bedding
  • Vacuum carpets and rugs
  • Dust furniture and surfaces
  • Check closets and storage
  • Clean mirrors and windows
  • Empty trash and replace liners
  • Arrange furniture properly

Living Areas

  • Vacuum furniture and cushions
  • Dust all surfaces and electronics
  • Clean coffee tables and side tables
  • Organize and stage decor
  • Vacuum and mop floors
  • Clean windows and mirrors
  • Check remote controls and manuals

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 48 hours in advance, but we can often accommodate same-day requests based on availability.

Do I need to be home during cleaning?

No, you don't need to be home. Many of our clients provide us with access and go about their day. We're fully insured and bonded for your peace of mind.

What if I'm not satisfied with the cleaning?

We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely happy with our service, we'll return within 24 hours to make it right.

Do you bring your own supplies?

Yes, we bring all necessary cleaning supplies and equipment. If you prefer us to use your products, just let us know when booking.
