Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 48 hours in advance, but we can often accommodate same-day requests based on availability.

No, you don't need to be home. Many of our clients provide us with access and go about their day. We're fully insured and bonded for your peace of mind.

What if I'm not satisfied with the cleaning?

We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely happy with our service, we'll return within 24 hours to make it right.

Do you bring your own supplies?

Yes, we bring all necessary cleaning supplies and equipment. If you prefer us to use your products, just let us know when booking.
